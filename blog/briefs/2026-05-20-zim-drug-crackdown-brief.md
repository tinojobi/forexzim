## CONTEXT NEEDED FOR READER

This operation is part of Zimbabwe's ongoing Multi-Sectoral Drug and Substance Abuse Plan for 2024-2030, which aims to disrupt illegal drug networks and tackle growing substance abuse among youths. The crackdown represents a significant escalation in enforcement efforts, combining law enforcement actions with social interventions like awareness campaigns, rehabilitation programmes, and economic empowerment initiatives.
