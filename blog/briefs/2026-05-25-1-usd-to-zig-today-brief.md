# Research brief: 1 USD to ZiG Today: Zimbabwe Exchange Rate Explained

## KEY FACTS
- [HIGH] ZiG, short for Zimbabwe Gold, was introduced by the RBZ as a structured currency in April 2024 and backed by foreign currency and precious metals, mainly gold. Sources: FactCheckZW, Statutory Instrument 60.
- [HIGH] The legal framework says ZiG must be issued against reserve assets held and managed by the RBZ. Source: Statutory Instrument 60.
- [HIGH] ZiG started at US$1 to 13.5616 ZiG. Source: FactCheckZW.
- [HIGH] ZWG is the ISO 4217 currency code for Zimbabwe Gold, numeric code 924, with two minor units. Source: SIX Group.
- [MEDIUM] RBZ publishes daily/interbank exchange-rate notices. Source: RBZ exchange-rates page.
