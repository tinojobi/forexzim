About these rates

Rates are sourced from the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe, local banks (CBZ, FBC), and market tracking sites (ZimPriceCheck).

ZiG is the common name and ZWG is the ISO 4217 code for the same currency — Zimbabwe Gold, introduced in April 2024.
