Frequently asked questions

What is the cheapest way to send money to Zimbabwe?

The cheapest option depends on where you're sending from and how much you're sending. Services with the lowest combined fee and best exchange rate give your recipient the most ZiG. Mama Money and Mukuru are typically strong for transfers from South Africa, while Remitly is competitive from the US and UK. Always compare the total your recipient receives, not just the fee.

How long does it take to send money to Zimbabwe?

Most major services — Mukuru, WorldRemit, Remitly — offer near-instant or same-day transfers to Zimbabwe. Mobile money delivery is usually fastest. Bank transfers can take 1–2 business days.

Can I send USD directly to Zimbabwe?

Yes. Most services allow you to send USD which is converted to ZiG at their exchange rate. Some services also support direct USD cash pickup, which is popular for recipients who prefer to hold dollars.

What exchange rate do remittance services use?

Remittance services set their own rates, based on the official USD/ZiG rate but with a built-in margin. This margin is how they make money even on low-fee transfers. The official rate is set by the Reserve Bank of Zimbabwe — you can track it live on ZimRate.

Is it safe to send money to Zimbabwe online?

All services listed here are licensed, regulated money transfer operators. Look for FCA (UK), FinCEN (US), or SARB (South Africa) licensing. Never send money via informal channels or unregulated apps.
